What is cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice that protects computers and servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data against malicious attacks. It is also referred to as information technology security or electronic information security. The term is applicable in a variety of contexts, from businesses to mobile computing.
